FC TamiLionS

"Creating Pathways to Success in Football"

HISTORY

Established in 2021 to promote football in Tamil Nadu.

Founded by Mr. Rajarajan, a former National football player.

Dedicated to nurturing young talent and enhancing football standards in the state.

MISSION

To groom and develop young football talent in Tamil Nadu.

Produce at least 20 players annually who will excel in the nation’s top leagues and represent the national team.

To create a structured pathway for players to reach the highest levels of football through world-class coaching and facilities.

COMMITMENT

To produce high-quality footballers who will represent Tamil Nadu at national and international levels.

To consistently nurture talent and provide world-class training and opportunities for players to reach their full potential.

To contribute to the growth of football in Tamil Nadu and strengthen the foundation for the national team.

VISION

  • To develop players from Tamil Nadu to meet the skills and competencies required at the national level.
  • To provide greater exposure and opportunities for aspiring young players to transition into professional football.
  • To nurture more talented players from Tamil Nadu who will represent India in top national leagues and the national team.

AIFF & STATE YOUTH LEAGUE

FC TamiLionS Accreditation Application

FC TamiLionS has applied for AIFF Academy Accreditation for the second consecutive year (2024-25). This accreditation will allow the academy to participate in the AIFF Youth Leagues and State Youth League.

AIFF Youth I-League Overview

The AIFF Youth I-League is India's premier youth football tournament, featuring age groups from U-13 to U-21. The league is organized by the AIFF and Tamil Nadu Football Association (TFA), the governing bodies for football at the national and state levels.

League Structure

The league operates on zonal and national levels, with teams from ISL and I-League clubs, as well as qualified academies. Matches are played on FIFA-standard grounds in a home-and-away format.

Media Coverage and Exposure

The league receives extensive media coverage, including regional and national outlets. Updates are shared through AIFF and I-League websites, social media, and live broadcasts, offering great visibility for players, academies, and sponsors.

Nationwide Player Exposure

The AIFF Youth I-League offers players the chance to compete against the best teams in India, providing vital exposure for their development and showcasing their talent.

Opportunities for Talented Players

Top performers from the league have opportunities to be scouted for state and national teams, representing their regions or the country in various competitions.
